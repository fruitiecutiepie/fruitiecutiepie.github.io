UTS Central

aka Building 2

UTS Central is a vibrant student hub and faculty space at the heart of our city campus. It's home to the new UTS Library and scholarly Reading Room, a wealth of student study spaces, and an inviting, publicly accessible food court that opens onto Alumni Green.

Why I like it:

  1. There are a lot of study spaces
  2. The view is nice
  3. I like going to UTS Library
  4. There is food court
  5. There is microwave (but no fridge unfortunately)
